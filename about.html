Who We Are

Kay Empire Group Sdn Bhd is a Malaysian-based company led by a husband-and-wife team, passionate about quality printing and memorable gift solutions.

Since 2017, we have built a strong reputation as one of Malaysia's most trusted wedding gift suppliers, serving over 10,000 brides across the country.

We specialize in custom printing, personalized wedding souvenirs, and corporate gifts. From beautifully crafted doorgifts for weddings to exclusive souvenirs for business events, we offer solutions that are thoughtful, affordable, and impactful.

Our Strength

With a workforce of more than 30 dedicated team members across multiple departments, we ensure that every order is handled professionally, with attention to detail and care.

Our streamlined process, reliable service, and commitment to customer satisfaction make us the preferred choice for thousands of brides and companies.
